Privacy Statement

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

LeaderRate collects only the information you provide: a generated codename, a password, and any feedback or updates you submit. No real names, email addresses, or contact information are stored.

2. How We Use Data

Your codename appears next to feedback you post. Feedback is public by default unless removed by moderators. Office updates are also public.

3. Anonymity & Safety

We log abuse signals (e.g., reports, duplicate submissions) to prevent spam, but we never attempt to identify individual users beyond their codename.

4. Data Retention

Feedback and updates remain visible until a moderator removes them. You may request deletion of your account by contacting the platform administrator.

5. Third Parties

We use Supabase as our database and authentication provider. No other third parties receive your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any changes will be posted here with a revised date.
